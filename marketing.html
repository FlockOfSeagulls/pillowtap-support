Three Breathing Patterns

4-7-8 Relaxing

Inhale 4s · Hold 7s · Exhale 8s

Developed by Dr. Andrew Weil specifically for sleep. The extended exhale activates your parasympathetic nervous system, telling your brain it's safe to rest.

Box Breathing

Inhale 4s · Hold 4s · Exhale 4s · Rest 4s

Used by Navy SEALs to stay calm under pressure. Equal phases create a steady rhythm that quiets racing thoughts.

Simple Relaxation

Inhale 4s · Exhale 6s

No hold phase, just a natural in-and-out flow. Perfect if you're new to breathing exercises or prefer simplicity.
