Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

Pillow Tap is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data whatsoever.

Data Collection

Pillow Tap does not collect any data. The app runs entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no network requests.

Personal Information

We do not collect, use, or share any personal information. Your breathing pattern preferences and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Pillow Tap does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Device Permissions

Pillow Tap uses your device's haptic engine (Taptic Engine) to provide vibration feedback. It also temporarily adjusts screen brightness during a session. No other device features or permissions are accessed.

Children's Privacy

Pillow Tap does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact
